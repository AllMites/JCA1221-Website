Glow Spec

  • Active: shadow-[0_0_24px_rgba(6,182,212,0.5)] cyan, border-cyan-500/80
  • Hovered: shadow-[0_0_16px_rgba(6,182,212,0.4)] cyan (slightly dimmer)
  • Locked: shadow-[0_0_28px_rgba(250,204,21,0.6)] amber (visual distinction)
  • Inactive: border border-white/10, no shadow, opacity 0.5
  • Connecting line: thin h-px between icons, inactive. No orb.
  • Transition: transition-all duration-300

Icon Source
